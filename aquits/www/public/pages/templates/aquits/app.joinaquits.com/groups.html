About
When you join a group, you will be updated as soon as that group is set to begin. Once there are enough people to start (a process that can take a few days, and in some cases more than a week), you will be contacted about the start date.
Groups run 60 minutes - with the time carefully structured to maximize opportunities for support, encouragement, motivation, accountability and collective learning. Although group members can use time to be accountable regarding sobriety, that is not required - and only invited if and when someone is ready. Extensive attention goes to ways to grow stronger or "Acquits" other areas of life that get at the roots of compulsive challenges like this.
Group members agree to follow particular guidelines for safety and confidentiality. In between group meetings, you will be free to communicate with any other members in the group during the week via messenger. Early on in the group, you will have an option of joining with two other people for a higher level of support and accountability. Each group will continue as long as there are enough people finding benefit to continue it.
FAQ
It Looks Like Some Groups Will Have To Wait A Little While Until They Have Enough Participants. Will My Credit Card Be Charged While I’m Waiting?
No it will not – not until the group starts.
What If The Group I Picked Never Has Enough Participants?
We would let you know that this particular group won’t be happening. In that case, you might need to consider another time that might work for you.
How Long Do You Expect A Group May Last?
We’ve seen similar groups last for many months – some even years – especially if group members find a powerful support and motivation together. If group members drop out, we will offer the spot to someone else. If the group does not have enough people to continue, a particular group may be discontinued, with space in other groups made available to those interested in continuing.
What Happens If I Can’t Make A Group Meeting?
Your group leader can catch you up a little on what you missed. Unfortunately, we cannot offer individual refunds for individual meetings missed. Your initial payment makes group meetings available for the period ahead.
What If I Change My Mind And Want To Cancel?
If group meetings aren’t helping enough or meeting your expectations, you are always welcome to cancel. No further group fee will show up on your account from the time you cancel.
