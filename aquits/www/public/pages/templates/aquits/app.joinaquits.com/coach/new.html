Format
When you sign up for coaching. You will have unlimited messaging with a recovery expert. It will be your responsibility to reach out to the Acquits experts when needed. Our coaches will respond within 24 hrs. You’ll be able to reach out anytime you need or want. The coach will be dedicated to helping support you in reaching the place you want to go.
FAQ
I’m Not Sure How Often I’ll Need My Coach. Is That Okay?
Of course. You can be as proactive as you’d like – and there’s no requirement for how often you need to connect (just whatever is best for you).
Are There Times When My Coach Won’t Be Available To Communicate?
Your coach will be available most days and times, but not all. One day each weekend will be a break day for them and they also need to sleep at night! (: If or when your coach goes on vacation, we’ll provide an option for you to connect with another person subbing for your coach.
Why Can’t I Talk To My Coach On The Phone?
While some coaching services use phone calls, they are naturally a great deal more expensive. In order to maximize access to personalized support for Acquits users, we have opted to launch first with this messaging support. A phone-based system is being discussed and planned for potential launch in 2018.
What If I Really Want A Coach That Is My Same Gender?
Totally fine! We’ll make sure that happens...just let us know. We’ve seen good success with mixed-gender coaching as well.
Can I Keep My Anonymity While Working With A Coach?
Yes, you never have to give your identity at any time. You can chose to be referred by your screen name if that is most comfortable for you.
