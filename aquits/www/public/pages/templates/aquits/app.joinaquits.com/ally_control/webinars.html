Format
When you show up at a webinar, the video-conferencing feature will feature the presenting speaker. A comment feature on the bottom will offer a chance for questions throughout. Each webinar will also be available in an archive after the presentation date.
FAQ
How long are webinars?
They are usually 1 hour but sometimes may be a little shorter or a little longer.
Is it possible to ask live questions to presenters?
Yes via the comment system.
How do you decide on webinar presenters? Is that something I could do sometimes?
Were always on the look-out for insightful, encouraging researchers and professionals in the area. Were also interested in featuring the stories of people who have had success in reaching some lasting freedom. If you fall in one of those categories, for sure send us a note! (info@joinfortify.com)
