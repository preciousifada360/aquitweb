Mental Diet Inventory
ESTIMATED TIME 7 mins
Inventories are focused explorations of certain areas of modern life that have been connected in some way (through both research and clinical observation) to a vulnerability to online compulsive-addictive behavior.  By raising awareness of the unique pattern of potential contributors to pornography struggles, the aim is to help you "get at the roots" of this problem in a way that helps you start making plans to unsettle and address these deeper influences.  In this way, we hope each inventory can help raise crucial awareness that guides you forward toward more and more lasting, sustainable freedom.   

Please answer these questions based on your experiences over the last 2-3 months - looking at patterns across this most recent period of your life (and NOT necessarily before).
